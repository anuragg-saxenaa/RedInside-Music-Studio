What Else Can Phase 4 Add?

Pick the features that matter most. Multi-select — choose as many as you want.

1

A/B Track Comparator

Select two versions of a track and listen to them side by side. Click to switch instantly. Essential for "which mix is better?" decisions. SoundCloud and Splice both have this.

💡 High impact — you have 3–5 versions of every track right now with no way to compare

2

Social Media Export Presets

One-click export clips: TikTok (60s), Instagram Reels (30s), YouTube Shorts (60s), Twitter (2:20). Artwork baked into the video frame. FFmpeg already handles this — just needs UI.

💡 High impact — turns the studio into a distribution tool, not just a creation tool

3

Smart Playlists + Auto-Tags

AI auto-detects BPM, key, mood, genre from audio. Smart playlists auto-populate: "All Punjabi tracks", "High energy >120 BPM", "Unmastered". Manual tags too. Your library becomes searchable and organised.

💡 High impact — directly solves "songs all over the place" problem

4

Track Notes + Timestamps

Leave notes pinned to specific timestamps on a track. "Fix the drop at 1:24", "Chorus needs more bass". Like SoundCloud comments but private. Shows on waveform as markers.

Medium impact — useful for iterative production workflow

5

Waveform Previews on Track Cards

Each track in the library shows a mini waveform instead of just a title. Visual fingerprint for each song — you instantly recognise tracks by shape. Click any part to seek. SoundCloud signature feature.

Medium impact — visual identity, makes the library feel premium

6

Share Links (Read-Only Project View)

Generate a link to share a project — collaborators see tracks, lyrics, artwork and can play everything. No login needed. Like Figma share links but for music. Token-protected URLs.

Medium impact — makes the studio shareable, useful for label pitches / collabs

7

AI Remix Suggestions

After generating a track, AI suggests 3 remix directions: "Slow it down + add reverb for lo-fi", "Speed up + add bass boost for gym", "Add echo for stadium feel". One-click applies the preset in Audio Editor.

Medium impact — creative discovery, reduces blank-page problem

8

Release Readiness Checklist

Before export, show a checklist: ✅ Mastered to -14 LUFS · ✅ Artwork added · ⚠ No lyrics attached · ⚠ Title is "Version 1". Blocks accidental release of unfinished tracks. Like Spotify for Artists pre-release checks.

Low effort, high professionalism signal

My Recommendation: Start With 3

1 + 2 + 3 together make the biggest UX leap with the least new backend work:

• A/B Compare — pure frontend, uses existing audio files, solves an immediate pain
• Social Export — FFmpeg already does this, just needs UI presets
• Smart Playlists + Auto-Tags — solves the "songs everywhere" problem directly

Items 4–8 can ship in a Phase 4.1 once the core workspace is stable.
